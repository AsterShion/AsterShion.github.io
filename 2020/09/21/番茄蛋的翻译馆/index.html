2020-09-21

What are some things that make you really sad?

I recently forgot my email password.

My security question (set many years ago) was “What do you want to be when you grow up?”

I’m unable to recollect my original answer…

I wonder how many others lost their way; and whether they ever found their way back to their dream.

EDIT:
I came across this recently, may bring a smile :)

最近我忘了自己的电邮密码。

安全问题（很多年前设置的）是“当你长大后想去做什么”

我已经无法回忆起当初的答案了……

不知多少人迷失了，能否找回来时的路，不忘初心。

编辑：
我最近偶然想起来了，笑:)

原文载于quora上，答者Abhinav Deshmukh对“What are some things that make you really sad?”的回答
https://qr.ae/pNH0XI

The billions of thoughts and memories and moments that perish with each human mind. Even a prolific writer could only record a small fraction of his or her thoughts.  When we die, an ocean of thought in our mind goes with us.

It’s pretty to me in a way, but also sad.

数以万计的想法、记忆、以及片段，它们伴随着大脑消亡。即使是一个多产的作家也只能记录下他想法中的一小部分。当我们死亡，无数的想法和我们一同消逝。

美而哀伤。
